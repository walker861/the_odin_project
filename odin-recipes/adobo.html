Chicken Adobo

This is a fan favorite of Filipino cuisine. I grew up making it in a slow cooker in a sweet and savory broth. I have tried other varieties where the sauce is more like a glaze. Honestly, either style is equally delicious over a bed of steamed white rice. Let's get started.

Recipe:

Ingredients

  • 6 pounds chicken thighs (bone-in)
  • 2 teaspoons whole black peppercorns
  • 8 cloves of garlic, smashed and peeled
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 2/3 cup of soy sauce
  • 2/3 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar

Directions

  1. (Optional) Brown chicken thighs in skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Place chicken thighs in the bottom of the slow cooker.
  3. Mix soy sauce, vinegar, brown sugar and stir to dissolve sugar. Add this mixture to the slow cooker.
  4. Add remaining ingredients: bay leaves, garlic, and peppercorns.
  5. Cook on High setting until chicken internal temperature is 165 degrees F.
  6. Enjoy over a bed of steamed white rice!
