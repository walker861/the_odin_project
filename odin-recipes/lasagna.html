Lasagna

Mom's spaghetti? Ok, Marshall. How about Mom's Lasagna?! Lasagna is a staple in the family dinner line up, tried and true. This simple meal is great for families large and small alike. What's the best part of a great lasagna? The leftovers make an easy lunch for the next day.

Recipe:

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 cup sliced black olives
  • 1 pinch of basil
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried sage
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon dried onion flakes
  • 2 (14 ounce) jars marinara sauce
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 8 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, brown ground beef and italian sausage. Drain excess grease.
  2. Stir in water, black olives, basil, garlic powder, oregano, sage, pepper, minced garlic, and onion flakes. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring frequently. Stir in the marinara sauce then remove from heat.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  4. Set aside 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese for later.Start your lasagna layers in a 9x13 inch casserole dish. Start with meat sauce in the bottom, a later of dry noodle next, then a thin layer of ricotta and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers ending with meat sauce on top.
  5. Bake for 30-45 minutes. Lasagna is cooked when you can easily stick a knife into it. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella on top, place back in the oven for 5-10 minutes to melt the top layer of cheese.
  6. Enjoy!
